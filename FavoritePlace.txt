Ahmedabad, located in the western state of Gujarat, is a vibrant city renowned for its rich cultural heritage and as the birthplace of Mahatma Gandhi.
With a blend of historical landmarks and modern developments, it offers a unique experience for visitors and residents alike.

Ahmedabad  is famous for its mouthwatering street food, including the iconic Gujarati snacks like dhokla and fafda. Ahmedabad also hosts the colorful and lively Kite Festival during Uttarayan, attracting kite enthusiasts from around the world to its skies.
